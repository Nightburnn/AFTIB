    Nestled on 155 acres of picturesque pasture & gently rolling land filled w/mature timber, this 3BR/2BA cabin offers a perfect retreat. Covered front/rear porches & lead to the carpet free cabin w/open plan. The great rm holds a stone fireplace & wood floors that flows into the eat-in kitchen w/bar area. Travel down the hall to discover 3BR/2BA, including a primary suite w/BA. The property also includes an oversized detached, 2-car garage w/10-ft doors, perfect for ample parking for all of your vehicles, extra storage, or a perfect workshop space. This idyllic getaway meets all your needs, whether for hunting, farming, livestock, horses, or recreation, w/numerous trails for hiking or ATVs. Enjoy the freedom of unrestricted land to create your dream retreat or any vision you have in mind. A peaceful stream winds through the property, a gated entry for max privacy, an abundance of wildlife & meticulously maintained pastures + a large pond on the upper field make this truly a one of a kind property w/a park-like setting. City utilities + broadband internet make working from home easy. Experience peaceful living on this beautiful farmland, conveniently located less than 10min from I-75
